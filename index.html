RULES

Objective

The objective of Connect Four is to be the first player to connect four of their own colored discs in a vertical, horizontal, or diagonal line on the game board.

How To Play

  • The player who makes the first move every round shifts each game so it is fare for both the players
  • The chosen disc is dropped from the top of the selected column, and it will fall to the lowest available position within that column.
  • Once a disc is placed, it cannot be moved or removed.
  • The game continues until one player successfully connects four of their own colored discs in a row.
  • The connection can be made vertically, horizontally, or diagonally in any direction.
  • If a player connects four discs and wins the game.
  • If the entire game board is filled with discs, and no player has connected four in a row, the game ends in a tie.
  • In the case of a tie, neither player wins, and the game is considered a draw.
